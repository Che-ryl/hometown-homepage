Visit Quilon

Enjoy culture and tradition in this vibrant district of Kerala, India.

Top three activities to do at Quilon

Are you taken aback by the huge size of this sculpture?

Jatayupara is the largest bird structure in the world which is linked to the Hindu epic, Ramayana.

Go for a boat ride amid a wonderful nature retreat

Munroe is home to 8 islands and the presence of shimmering Lake Ashtamudi and Lake Kallada on either side of it makes it special.

Peer towards the Arabian Sea from a landmark lighthouse

Tangasseri Lighthouse was built by the British in 1902 and is the tallest lighthouse on the Kerala coast.
